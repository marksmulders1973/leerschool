Engels eindexamen VMBO-GL/TL 2023 tijdvak 2 — vraag 1 van 6

Which of the following becomes clear about the Ingham Family from this text?

Antwoordopties:
  1. The baby doll they are offering for sale has been met with negative reactions. ✓
  2. The baby doll they created is especially designed to help childless couples.
  3. They gained many new followers of their channel after the birth of their son.
  4. They have promised to donate the money they earn with their products to charity.
Het juiste antwoord is A: The baby doll they are offering for sale has been met with negative reactions.

'The announcement drew hostile comments' + voorbeelden ('Black Mirror', 'imagine finding out your parents sold dolls of you'). De doll-verkoop ontvangt negatieve reacties. Antwoord A.

Stap voor stap uitgelegd

  1. Kern

    Vraag 'becomes clear' = wat blijkt? Zoek conclusie-zinnen. Hier: 'hostile comments' + voorbeeld 'imagine your parents sold dolls of you' = duidelijk negatief. Andere opties verzinnen iets dat NIET in de tekst staat.
