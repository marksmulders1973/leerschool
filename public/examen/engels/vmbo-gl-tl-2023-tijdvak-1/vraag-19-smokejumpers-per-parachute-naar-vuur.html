Engels eindexamen VMBO-GL/TL 2023 tijdvak 1 — vraag 19 van 6

What is special about the smokejumpers, according to paragraph 1?

Antwoordopties:
  1. They are asked to join the brigade because they like heights.
  2. They combine their fire-fighting activities with a regular job.
  3. They go to areas that can only be reached through the air. ✓
  4. They like being in charge under difficult circumstances.
Het juiste antwoord is C: They go to areas that can only be reached through the air.

Alinea 1: 'They parachute out of planes to quickly reach fire sites that do not have roads — the only way in is from the air.' Smokejumpers gaan naar plaatsen die alleen vanuit de lucht bereikbaar zijn. = C.

Stap voor stap uitgelegd

  1. Kern

    'Fire sites that do not have roads — the only way in is from the air.' = vuur-locaties zonder wegen, alleen per lucht bereikbaar. Dat maakt smokejumpers uniek — gewone brandweer kan niet komen.
