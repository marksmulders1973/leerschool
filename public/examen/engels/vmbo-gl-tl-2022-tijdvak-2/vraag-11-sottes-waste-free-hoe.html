Engels eindexamen VMBO-GL/TL 2022 tijdvak 2 — vraag 11 van 6

'Sottes created waves when it launched its waste-free collection' (paragraph 1). How does Sottes produce a waste-free collection?

Antwoordopties:
  1. by developing fabrics made from organic litter and plastic
  2. by making new clothes from materials that have been thrown away ✓
  3. by selecting local materials instead of textiles produced abroad
  4. by using fair trade methods throughout the whole production process
Het juiste antwoord is B: by making new clothes from materials that have been thrown away

Tekst: 'upcycling, a process in which unwanted pieces of fabric are transformed' + 'waste fabric collected from a community shop'. = oude/weggegooide materialen omgevormd. Antwoord B.

Stap voor stap uitgelegd

  1. Kern

    Upcycling = oude stof tot iets MOOIERS verwerken. Niet recycling (vermalen tot grondstof) maar TRANSFORMEREN. 'Waste fabric collected from community shop' = stof die mensen weggooiden.
