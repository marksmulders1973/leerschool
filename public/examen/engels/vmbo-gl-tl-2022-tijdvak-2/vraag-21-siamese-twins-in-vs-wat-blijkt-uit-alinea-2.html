What becomes clear from paragraph 2 of 'The story of the original Siamese twins'?

Antwoordopties:
  1. The show of the twins became popular enough to fill big venues.
  2. The show of the twins sometimes ended in public fights and chaos.
  3. The twins reacted in an identical way to being publicly exhibited.
  4. The twins were interesting to various people for various reasons. ✓
Het juiste antwoord is D: The twins were interesting to various people for various reasons.

Alinea 2 lijst diverse publiek-groepen + REDENEN: 'people gawped' (curiositeit), 'medical professionals studied' (medisch), 'rich patrons dined' (status), 'reformers debated moral implications' (ethiek). = diverse mensen, diverse redenen. Antwoord D.

Stap voor stap uitgelegd

  1. Kern

    Alinea lijst 4 GROEPEN met 4 verschillende REDENEN: gewone mensen (gawped = staarden), artsen (medisch), rijke mensen (dineren), hervormers (moraal). Slot-zin: 'audience as varied as their hosts'. = divers publiek, diverse redenen.
