Engels eindexamen VMBO-GL/TL 2022 tijdvak 1 — vraag 6 van 6

'with American children topping the rankings' (paragraph 2). Which possible reason for this top ranking is mentioned in paragraph 2?

Antwoordopties:
  1. American schools tend to start even earlier than schools in Great Britain. ✓
  2. American society is very competitive and focused on scoring high.
  3. In America schooldays are often longer than eight hours a day.
  4. In America there is more research into sleep going on than elsewhere.
Het juiste antwoord is A: American schools tend to start even earlier than schools in Great Britain.

Alinea 2: 'School in the US typically starts even BEFORE 8am, considerably EARLIER than in many other Western countries.' UK-onderzoek vergelijkt met UK-school (8:30) — VS is dus nog vroeger = nog meer slaaptekort. Antwoord A.

Stap voor stap uitgelegd

  1. Kern

    Vraag 'which reason mentioned in paragraph 2' = scan ALLEEN alinea 2 voor reden. Sleutelwoord 'even before 8am, considerably earlier'. Geen 'competitive' of 'longer day' in tekst — die zijn verzonnen verleiders.

Wat moet je weten om deze vraag te kunnen beantwoorden?

    begrijpen 'sleep-deprived', 'ranking', 'developed nations'
