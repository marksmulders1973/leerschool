'At first I was confused' (paragraph 3). Why was Joe Nicchi confused?

Antwoordopties:
  1. He found it hard to believe that so many young people were interested in his ice cream.
  2. He was startled by the great number of strangers that reacted to his Instagram.
  3. He was surprised by how many people preferred his ice cream over more expensive ones.
  4. He wondered why people wanted something for free that costs very little to make. ✓
Het juiste antwoord is D: He wondered why people wanted something for free that costs very little to make.

Joe's quote: 'a $4 cone costs me 30 cents to make — pay me, that's how this works.' Hij vroeg zich af: waarom willen mensen GRATIS iets dat zo goedkoop te maken is + waarom verwachten ze dat als prominentie-claim genoeg is? Antwoord D.

Stap voor stap uitgelegd

  1. Kern

    Joe's confusion zit in de combo: kostprijs $0.30, verkoopprijs $4, en influencer eist het GRATIS — alleen op claim van 100k volgers. Joe ziet dat niet als business: 'pay me, that's how this works'.

Wat moet je weten om deze vraag te kunnen beantwoorden?

  1. Woordenschat Engels VMBO-GT eindexamen
    begrijpen 'confused', 'influencer', 'cone', 'free'
  2. Begrijpend lezen strategie po-2F
    waarom-vraag + match met quote in tekst — kern-truc
