Engels eindexamen VMBO-GL/TL 2022 tijdvak 1 — vraag 9 van 6

What becomes clear from paragraphs 1 and 2 of 'Heavy Dining'?

Antwoordopties:
  1. A number of restaurants in the UK misinform consumers about the ingredients.
  2. A publication about the quality of restaurants and fast-food chains turned out wrong.
  3. An undercover investigation has exposed wrongdoings in the UK hospitality business.
  4. Many meals served in restaurants contain more calories than meals served in fast-food chains. ✓
Het juiste antwoord is D: Many meals served in restaurants contain more calories than meals served in fast-food chains.

Alinea 1: 'sit-down restaurants are unhealthier than fast-food chains'. Alinea 2: 'Sit-down restaurants were five times more likely to offer high-calorie meals of 1,000kcal or more than fast-food restaurants.' → meer calorieën in restaurants. Antwoord D.

Stap voor stap uitgelegd

  1. Kern

    Lees alinea 1+2 + zoek de KERN. Hier: sit-down restaurants ≠ fast-food — sit-down BLIJKT 5× ongezonder. Andere opties verzinnen iets dat NIET in tekst staat.

Wat moet je weten om deze vraag te kunnen beantwoorden?

  1. Woordenschat Engels VMBO-GT eindexamen
    begrijpen 'sit-down', 'fast-food chain', 'kcal', 'recommended'
  2. Begrijpend lezen strategie po-2F
    hoofdgedachte 2 alinea's destilleren — kern-truc
