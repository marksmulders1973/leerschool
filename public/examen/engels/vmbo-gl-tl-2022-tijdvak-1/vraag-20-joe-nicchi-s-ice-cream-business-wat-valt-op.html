What becomes clear about Joe Nicchi's business in paragraphs 1 and 2?

Antwoordopties:
  1. His old-fashioned ice cream truck is trending on social media.
  2. His unusual and unique ice cream flavours are very popular.
  3. It is difficult for him to make a living with just selling ice cream.
  4. The concept of his ice cream business is quite traditional. ✓
Het juiste antwoord is D: The concept of his ice cream business is quite traditional.

Alinea 1: 'kept it simple' + 'vintage 1960s Mister Softee truck'. Alinea 2: 'aggressively simple, old-style options — chocolate, vanilla or twist'. = traditioneel concept. Andere opties verzinnen kenmerken die NIET in tekst staan. Antwoord D.

Stap voor stap uitgelegd

  1. Kern

    Sleutelwoorden alinea 1+2: 'simple', 'vintage 1960s', 'old-style', 'CVT = chocolate vanilla twist'. Alles wijst op TRADITIONEEL. Verleiders verzinnen 'trending' of 'unique flavours' die juist het tegenovergestelde zijn van wat in tekst staat.
