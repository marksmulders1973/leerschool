What is the function of paragraph 2?

Antwoordopties:
  1. to explain the consequences of the change in pay
  2. to illustrate how the change in pay was received ✓
  3. to show why the change in pay was inevitable
Het juiste antwoord is B: to illustrate how the change in pay was received

Paragraph 2 quotes Marta ('moment of joy, historic moment') and mentions fans applauding on social media. This shows the RECEPTION of the announcement — how people reacted.

Stap voor stap uitgelegd

  1. Reactie van mensen

    Alinea 2: Marta-citaat (joy/historic) + fans applaudisseren = REACTIES op het besluit. → B (how received).

Wat moet je weten om deze vraag te kunnen beantwoorden?

  1. Woordenschat po-1F
    alinea-functie: citaten + reacties = how something is received
  2. Begrijpend lezen — strategie po-1F
    verschil tussen 'gevolgen', 'reactie', 'waarom nodig' — eliminatie via inhoud alinea
