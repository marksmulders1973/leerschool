How is paragraph 2 linked to the discovery introduced in paragraph 1?

Antwoordopties:
  1. Paragraph 2 describes the emotional impact of the discovery.
  2. Paragraph 2 explains why the discovery drew a lot of attention.
  3. Paragraph 2 highlights the historical significance of the discovery.
  4. Paragraph 2 presents the facts of the discovery in more detail. ✓
Het juiste antwoord is D: Paragraph 2 presents the facts of the discovery in more detail.

Paragraph 2 quotes Allyn Gore directly + adds details: 'a small opening tucked beneath the ground... opened up into a small chamber, then a longer tunnel.' Pure factual elaboration on what was found.

Stap voor stap uitgelegd

  1. Quote = details

    Alinea 2 quote Allyn Gore + opening → chamber → tunnel = details over wat gevonden werd. → D (facts in detail).
