Engels eindexamen VMBO-GL/TL 2024 tijdvak 1 — vraag 6 van 8

What becomes clear in paragraph 1?

Antwoordopties:
  1. A group of workers came across something quite unforeseen. ✓
  2. A researcher stumbled upon a hidden entrance to a historic location.
  3. An ancient rumour about a secret underpass turned out to be true.
  4. An inspection of an old monastery caused quite some commotion.
Het juiste antwoord is A: A group of workers came across something quite unforeseen.

Paragraph 1 says: 'A routine excavation yielded something surprising when crews unearthed a mysterious medieval tunnel.' Workers (crews) doing routine work found something they didn't expect = unforeseen discovery.

Stap voor stap uitgelegd

  1. Crews + surprising = unforeseen

    'Crews unearthed something surprising' = werkers vonden iets onverwachts → optie A (group of workers, unforeseen).

Wat moet je weten om deze vraag te kunnen beantwoorden?

  1. Woordenschat po-1F
    NL-strategie 'synoniem matchen' — 'crews' = workers, 'surprising' = unforeseen
  2. Begrijpend lezen — strategie po-1F
    afleider 'researcher' versus tekst 'crews' — letterlijk lezen, niet aannemen
  3. Woordenschat Engels vmbo-3
    EN-vocab: 'crews', 'unforeseen', 'yielded', 'unearthed' — kern van deze examenvraag
