What can be concluded about the tunnel from paragraph 4?

Antwoordopties:
  1. It is expected to become a tourist attraction.
  2. It is still unclear when or why it was built. ✓
  3. It proves that people used to be a lot shorter.
  4. It seems to be damaged beyond repair.
Het juiste antwoord is B: It is still unclear when or why it was built.

Paragraph 4: 'The exact age and purpose remain mysterious... no historical record mentions it. Researchers say more excavation is needed before they can say when it was built or what it was used for.' Pure uncertainty.

Stap voor stap uitgelegd

  1. Mysterious = onbekend

    Alinea 4: 'age and purpose remain mysterious' = leeftijd én doel zijn nog onbekend. → B (still unclear when or why).

Wat moet je weten om deze vraag te kunnen beantwoorden?

  1. Woordenschat po-1F
    conclusie uit een alinea trekken — wat zegt de tekst LETTERLIJK over het object
  2. Begrijpend lezen — strategie po-1F
    synoniem-koppeling: 'mysterious age + purpose' = 'unclear when + why'
