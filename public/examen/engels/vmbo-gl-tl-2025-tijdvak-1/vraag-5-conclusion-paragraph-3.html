What can be concluded about electric vehicles from paragraph 3?

Antwoordopties:
  1. It is safer to park and recharge them outdoors. ✓
  2. Their fragility causes unsafe traffic situations.
  3. They tend to fall apart under rough conditions.
Het juiste antwoord is A: It is safer to park and recharge them outdoors.

Paragraph 3 says one of the issues is that LEVs 'are often stored or charged inside a home or workplace, so there is a higher risk of a fire spreading.' Conclusion: charging OUTSIDE (where fire can't spread to a building) is safer.

Stap voor stap uitgelegd

  1. Binnen risk → buiten safer

    Tekst: binnen opladen = brand spreidt. Conclusie: BUITEN opladen is veiliger. → A.
