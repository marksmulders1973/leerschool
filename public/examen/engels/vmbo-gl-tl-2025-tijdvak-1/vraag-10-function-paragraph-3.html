What is the function of paragraph 3?

Antwoordopties:
  1. to emphasise why research on microplastics is indispensable
  2. to explain what is going to be studied in the new research lab ✓
  3. to give an overview of the results and implications of the research
  4. to praise the scientists who conducted previous research projects
  5. to stress that more funding is needed for future research projects
Het juiste antwoord is B: to explain what is going to be studied in the new research lab

Paragraph 3 describes the TWO PROJECTS the UQ team will start: (1) measure microplastics in blood/urine/brain, (2) investigate small microplastics behavior. Pure description of what will be studied in the new lab.

Stap voor stap uitgelegd

  1. Twee projecten = wat onderzocht wordt

    Alinea 3 noemt 2 projecten (microplastic-niveau meten + gedrag onderzoeken). → B (explain what will be studied).

Wat moet je weten om deze vraag te kunnen beantwoorden?

  1. Woordenschat po-1F
    alinea-functie: beschrijven wat onderzocht wordt vs. argumenteren waarom belangrijk
  2. Begrijpend lezen — strategie po-1F
    alinea typeren: toekomstige projecten = wat wordt onderzocht; niet resultaten of funding
  3. Woordenschat Engels vmbo-3
    EN-vocab: 'investigate', 'ready to go', 'projects' — kern van deze examenvraag
