How is the topic of the article introduced in paragraph 1?

Antwoordopties:
  1. by claiming that most people can afford to buy an e-scooter
  2. by making clear that e-scooters are popular products ✓
  3. by outlining why bicycles are often replaced by e-scooters
  4. by stressing that e-scooters have become too cheap
Het juiste antwoord is B: by making clear that e-scooters are popular products

Paragraph 1 says: 'The largest share of that figure was in e-scooter sales, due to their cost effectiveness.' This shows e-scooters are POPULAR products with a big market share. The article then goes on to discuss problems.

Stap voor stap uitgelegd

  1. Largest share = popular

    'Largest share of revenue was in e-scooter sales' = grootste verkoop = populair product. → B.
