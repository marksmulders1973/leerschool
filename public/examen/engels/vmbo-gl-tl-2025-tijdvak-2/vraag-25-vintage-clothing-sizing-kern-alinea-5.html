What is the main point made in paragraph 5?

Antwoordopties:
  1. In the past there was no reliable way to determine sizes.
  2. It has become fashionable to wear more slim-fitting clothes.
  3. The sizing scale has gradually changed over the years. ✓
  4. Today's women are taller than those of previous generations.
Het juiste antwoord is C: The sizing scale has gradually changed over the years.

Alinea 5: *'A modern size 10 dress would have been called a size 14 in the 1960s and a size 16 in the 1940s. ... the numerical scale has gradually shifted.'* Het hoofdpunt: de maten-schaal is door de jaren heen veranderd — wat nu maat 10 heet, was vroeger maat 14 of 16. Optie C zegt dat letterlijk: *'sizing scale has gradually changed over the years.'*

Stap voor stap uitgelegd

  1. Hoofdgedachte vinden

    Lees alinea 5 helemaal. De kern-zin staat vaak vooraan (topic sentence) of als conclusie. Hier: *'A modern size 10 ... a size 14 in the 1960s ... a size 16 in the 1940s.'* Plus: *'the numerical scale has gradually shifted.'*

  2. Match opties

    Optie C 'sizing scale has gradually changed' = directe parafrase van 'numerical scale has gradually shifted'. Synoniemen: scale = schaal, gradually = geleidelijk, changed/shifted = veranderd/verschoven.

  3. Andere opties uitsluiten

    A: tekst zegt niet 'unreliable' — alleen 'changed'. B: geen slim-fit-mode-discussie. D: 'taller' niet genoemd — wel body shape + vanity sizing.

Wat moet je weten om deze vraag te kunnen beantwoorden?

  1. Woordenschat Engels po-1F
    begrijpen 'sizing', 'scale', 'gradually shifted', 'vanity sizing'
