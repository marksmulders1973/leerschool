Engels eindexamen VMBO-GL/TL 2025 tijdvak 2 — vraag 3 van 6

Why did Joan ask her aunt Adell to sign up for the spelling contest again, according to the text?

Antwoordopties:
  1. because Joan could not participate in the competition herself
  2. because Joan wanted to help the school to raise more money
  3. because she was a bit fed up with Adell's continuous boasting ✓
  4. because she was certain that Adell was still up to the task
Het juiste antwoord is C: because she was a bit fed up with Adell's continuous boasting

Sleutel-zin alinea 2: *'Joan ... admits she was a little tired of hearing her aunt boast about her unbeaten record.'* En: *'So I said: prove it.'* Joan was de eeuwige opscheppen-verhalen zat → uitdaagde Adell om het te bewijzen. 'Fed up with boasting' = klaar met opschepperij = optie C.

Stap voor stap uitgelegd

  1. Zoek het motief

    Vraag: waarom vroeg Joan Adell? Zoek in tekst een zin met 'because / tired of / so'.

  2. Sleutel-zin

    *'Joan admits she was a little tired of hearing her aunt boast'* + *'I said: prove it.'* = Joan was Adell's opscheppen zat → wilde bewijs.

  3. Wat is 'fed up'?

    Fed up with X = klaar met X / geen zin meer in X. 'Tired of' is synoniem. Optie C zegt 'fed up with continuous boasting' — directe parafrase.

Wat moet je weten om deze vraag te kunnen beantwoorden?
